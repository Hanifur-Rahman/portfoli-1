About Us

Levi Strauss & Co. is an American clothing company known worldwide for its Levi's brand of denim jeans. It was founded in May 1853[5] when German immigrant Levi Strauss moved from Buttenheim, Bavaria, to San Francisco, California to open a west coast branch of his brothers' New York dry goods business. Although the corporation is registered in Delaware,[6] the company's corporate headquarters is located in Levi's Plaza in San Francisco.
